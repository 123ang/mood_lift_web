1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website.

They help websites function properly and improve user experience by remembering certain information about your visit.

Cookies do not give us access to your device or personal files.

  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored
